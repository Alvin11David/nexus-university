## Important Information

### Overdue Assignments âš ï¸

- If an assignment is pending but the due date has passed, you'll see a **red alert**
- Try to submit as soon as possible (late submission may have penalties)

### Points Information

- Each assignment shows the **total points** possible
- When graded, your **actual score** will be shown
- Example: "8/10" means you scored 8 points out of 10

### Lecturer Feedback

- After your assignment is graded, you'll see:
  - Your score
  - Any comments or feedback from your lecturer
  - Suggestions for improvement

## Troubleshooting

### Document won't download?

- Check your internet connection
- Try a different browser
- Ensure you have space on your device

### Can't see an assignment?

- Make sure you're enrolled in that course
- Refresh the page
- Check if the course is active

### Need to submit?

- The **"Submit Assignment"** button is in the detail modal
